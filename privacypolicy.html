1. Our Vision of Digital Privacy

At Holisticpreneur Social, we believe your data is an extension of your energy. Unlike traditional social networks that harvest information for profit, we collect data solely to facilitate meaningful connections and human evolution.

This policy applies to all our services, including the mobile app, web platform, and integrated community tools.

2. The Data We Safeguard

To operate a high-performance social network, we collect several layers of information:

  • Account Credentials: Full name, verified email, and encrypted passwords.
  • Social Graph: Information about the people, groups, and hashtags you follow and interact with.
  • Metadata: To ensure platform stability, we log IP addresses, browser types, and session duration.
  • Private Communications: Messages sent through our platform are stored using secure protocols to prevent unauthorized access.

3. How Our Algorithms Use Your Data

Our feed algorithm is designed for "Conscious Curation." It uses your interactions to show you content that aligns with your growth goals. We do not use psychological triggers to increase "screen time" at the cost of your mental health.

4. Third-Party Ecosystem Flow

We do not sell your personal data. Period. However, we do share minimal data with service providers who help us run the site:

  • Cloud Infrastructure: Secure servers where your encrypted data resides.
  • Analytics: Aggregated, non-identifiable data to help us fix bugs and improve UI.
  • Legal Compliance: We only disclose data if required by a valid legal order or to protect the safety of our community members.

5. Data Retention Policy

We keep your data as long as your account is active. If you choose to leave the sanctuary of Holisticpreneur Social, we provide a 30-day grace period where your data is hidden but recoverable. After 30 days, your profile is permanently purged from our active databases.

6. Encryption & Safety Standards

We safeguard your digital presence by storing all personal information in a high-security Digital Vault, ensuring your data remains private and guarded. Your private conversations are shielded through End-to-End Scrambling, making them unreadable to anyone except the intended recipient.

7. Your Global Digital Rights

Regardless of your location, we honor high standards of privacy (GDPR, CCPA, and Digital Personal Data Protection Act):

  • Right to Portability: You can download a full archive of your posts and connections.
  • Right to be Forgotten: You can request total deletion of your digital footprint.
  • Right to Rectification: You have full control to edit any information you've shared.
